## static on the frequency | June 2
He finds the frequency by accident, at 2am, static and something almost like a voice underneath it.

He almost doesn't believe it at first. Radios don't work like that, not really, not the way this one seems to.

## everything the radio couldn't say | June 14
She starts leaving the radio on. Just in case. Not that she'd admit that's why.

Some nights the static says nothing at all. Some nights it says more than either of them are ready for.

## a station that isn't listed | June 29
The station isn't on any map. Neither, it turns out, is he.

She goes looking anyway.
